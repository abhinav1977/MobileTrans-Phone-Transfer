1. Open the MobileTrans software on your Windows computer.

3. Connect both source and destination phones to your computer using USB cables.
4. Wait for the software to detect your devices. You see the device names on the screen.
5. Choose the types of data you want to move. Select photos, messages, or files.
6. Click the start button to begin the transfer.

8. Disconnect your phones once the process finishes.

## 🔧 Troubleshooting Common Issues

If the software does not see your phone, try these steps.

- Unplug the USB cable and plug it into a different port on your computer.
- Use a different USB cable to check for cord damage.
- Check if your phone asks for permission to trust the computer. Select yes or allow.
- Restart your computer and the mobile phone.
- Ensure your phone drivers are updated on your Windows computer.

## 🛡 Performance and Safety

Your data remains safe during the transfer. The software reads data from the source phone and writes it to the destination phone.
